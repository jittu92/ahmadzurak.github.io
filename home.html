About Us


Welcome to Ahmad Jurak Pvt. Ltd. We have been serving shisha with heavenly fumes from the last four generations. Ahamd Jurak Pvt. Ltd. came into existence in the Year 1990 and we are celebrating 28 years of continued service to the Shisha Industry with a committment towards bringing most trendy and fashionable updates from time to time to cater ever changing need of our shisha enthusiasts.

A most notable recent update in our products range is introduction of flavoured tobacco, under our home brand "Mazaj". Ahmad Jurak is an acclaimed Export House and awarded as the second best exporter for achieving highest performance in exporting Hookah Tobacco Paste from Tobacco Board. We commit ourselves to offer the best quality shisha molasses for a heavenly shisha experience and we are determined to carry forward the legacy in the future.
